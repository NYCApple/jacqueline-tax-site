Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to start my return?

Typically W‑2, 1099s, prior‑year returns, receipts for deductions, your ID, and any pertinent forms like 1098‑T or 1098‑E.

Is my information safe when uploaded to the portal?

Yes. All files are encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is protected with secure logins and optional MFA.

Can I sign my tax documents online?

Yes. We use IRS‑compliant electronic signatures for a fast and secure experience.

Do you accept both individuals and small businesses?

Yes. We serve individuals, families, and businesses of all sizes.

How long does it take to prepare my return?

Most individual returns are completed within 5–7 business days after receiving all documents.
